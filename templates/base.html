Welcome to Social Media Sentiment Analysis

This platform harnesses the power of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and machine learning to interpret public opinion by analyzing sentiments from social media posts.

Sentiment analysis plays a crucial role in today’s digital landscape. By examining user-generated content, this tool helps uncover hidden trends in emotions, attitudes, and behaviors—essential for brands, researchers, and policymakers to understand their audience better.

This project is inspired by the research paper “Social Media Sentiment Analysis: A Comprehensive Analysis”, which highlights the importance of opinion mining and the challenges of analyzing informal, multilingual, and emotive data from social media platforms.
